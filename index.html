~ Our goal is to give you the best roleplay experience.

Welcome to Extreme Roleplays! We're a passionate community of roleplayers, here to bring your creative ideas to life. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, we offer a variety of fun and immersive roleplaying experiences for everyone. Join us and explore new adventures!

Extreme Roleplays is founded by Noob1e - "I used to work at an ER:LC roleplay community with all my effort. But the founder of the server did not care. Not only me, he mistreated and punished everybody unreasonably. Unreasonable demotions, strikes, termination, unfair promotions, etc.
I wanted to stop this unfairness. I wanted to give every staff a chance to work and show their effort towards the server. I will provide everyone with 'extreme'-ly amazing roleplays.
I could bring this to reality with my amazing friends: Mr. Man [Co-Founder], Adam [CEO], Justin [EO], Lewis [EO], Dani [EO]"
